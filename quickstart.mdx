---
title: 'Quickstart'
description: 'Set up Stacks for your personal use in less than two minutes'
---

<Steps>
    <Step title="Download the extension">
        Stacks starts with the browser extension. Head to the browser extension store and download the extension for your
        browser. Once you have the extension installed, you can start capturing and organizing information on the web.

        <AccordionGroup>
            <Accordion icon="chrome" title="Download for Chrome (Chromium)">
                [Download the Chrome
                extension](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/save-and-search-with-stac/nfjbjlpkfimhobegkcoekpkdlokjkcfj)

                Install the Chrome extension for any Chromium-based browser like Brave, Edge, or Vivaldi and proceed to the
                next step.
            </Accordion>

            <Accordion icon="firefox" title="Download for Firefox">
                Firefox extension is coming soon. Stay in touch to get notified when it's available.
            </Accordion>
        </AccordionGroup>
    </Step>

    <Step title="Sign up for Stacks">
        Once you have the extension installed, sign up for Stacks using your email address. You can also sign up using
        your Google account. Open your extension and click on the sign-up button to create your account.
    </Step>

    <Step title="Import your bookmarks (optional)">
        [Import your bookmarks](https://app.betterstacks.com/import) to Stacks if you have bookmarks saved in your
        browser.

        Alternatively open the extension and click on the
        import bookmarks button to import your bookmarks to Stacks.
    </Step>

    <Step title="Ready to go!">
        You are all set! Start capturing and organizing information on the web using Stacks. If you need any help, you
        can always refer to the [documentation](/introduction).
    </Step>
</Steps>
