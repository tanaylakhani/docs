Stacks allows you to easily import your saved links from a CSV file, helping you consolidate all your bookmarks into one platform for better organisation and access.

### Steps to Import Links Using a CSV File

Follow these steps to import your links from a CSV file into Stacks:

1. **Prepare Your CSV File**:
    - Ensure your CSV file contains the saved links you want to import. The file can include additional information like titles if available.
2. **Access the Stacks Import Feature**:
    - Open the **Stacks Dashboard** and sign in to your account.
    - Click on the **Import Links** option from the dropdown menu in the top right corner.
3. **Upload the CSV File**:
    - You will be prompted to upload a file. Choose the prepared **CSV** file from your computer.
4. **Start the Import Process**:
    - Click **Upload** to begin importing your links into Stacks.
5. **View Imported Links**:
    - The imported links will appear in your Stacks Dashboard, organised and ready for use. You can further categorise them into collections as needed.
