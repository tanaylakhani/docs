The Stacks AI feature on Stacks transforms how you interact with your saved content. It allows you to gain insights, brainstorm ideas, and perform quick tasks using AI directly within your dashboard.

### Introducing Stacks AI

1. **Locate Stacks AI**:
   - On the left-hand side of the Stacks dashboard, find the **Stacks AI** option.
   - Click to open the chat interface.

2. **Purpose**:
   - Chat AI is designed to provide insights, summarize content, and assist with tasks related to your saved links and resources.

### Chatting About a Saved Article

1. **Open a Saved Article**:
   - Select any saved article from your collections or dashboard.
   - Click on the "+" sign next to the article to add it to the context.

2. **Ask AI Questions**:
   - Use the Stacks AI to ask questions related to the article.
   - Example queries include:
     - *"Summarize this article for me."*
     - *"Explain the key concepts in simple terms."*
     - *"Suggest related resources or readings."*

3. **Interactive Responses**:
   - The AI will generate responses directly linked to the content, providing useful insights and summaries.

### Practical Applications

- **Drafting Emails**: Use the AI to draft emails based on the content of the article.
- **Brainstorming**: Get ideas and suggestions for projects or tasks inspired by the saved content.
- **Research Support**: Ask for references, related topics, or additional material to deepen your understanding.

### Why Use Stacks AI?

- **Efficiency**: Save time by letting the AI summarize and explain content quickly.
- **Insightful**: Gain deeper knowledge and explore related ideas without leaving the dashboard.
- **Productivity**: Streamline workflows by interacting directly with saved articles through AI.
