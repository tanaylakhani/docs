Stacks enables you to upload and store PDF files, allowing you to keep important documents, articles, and notes organised in one platform. This feature makes it easy to access your PDFs from anywhere and view them directly within Stacks.

### Steps to Upload PDF Files

Follow these steps to upload your PDF files to Stacks:

1. **Open the Stacks Dashboard**:
    - Sign in to your Stacks account and navigate to the **Dashboard**.
2. **Access the Upload Feature**:
    - Click on the **Upload File** button, usually found in the top right corner of the Dashboard.
3. **Select Your PDF File**:
    - In the file picker window, choose the **PDF** file you want to upload from your computer.
4. **Upload the File**:
    - Click **Upload** to start the process. You will see a progress bar indicating the upload status.
5. **View and Organise**:
    - Once uploaded, the PDF file will appear in your Stacks Dashboard. You can view the file directly, add it to a collection, or leave notes for better organisation.
